UK fire safety regulations recommend that fire alarm systems be serviced at least every six months by a qualified technician. However, high-risk premises may require more frequent checks. Regular servicing ensures:
  • Compliance with BS 5839 fire safety standards.
  • Early detection of faults to prevent system failures.
  • Extended lifespan of your fire alarm system.
